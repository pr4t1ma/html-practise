our story.

how we spend our days is how we live our lives; and we spend our days having fun.

NEW stands for Never Ending Weekends: it’s both our motto and the way we live our lives. We are the best design paired with the best thinkers in coding and communications. We don’t look back but we learn from our past. We’re young, bright and keep close tabs on the industry to ensure you are always getting the most up to date advice, design and knowledge. We have fun, but we’re invested in everything we do, and if you’re with us, we’re investing in you too. We are NEW and we’re a creative digital agency.

services

everyone has a unique story, and we're here to help tell yours.

management

Newgroup will manage your project through strategising and planning to always make sure we see from your point view; meaning quality assurance, monitoring and tracking throughout/from start to finish.

creative

Our creative team produce: branding, websites, logos, visual identity, stationery, marketing collateral, packaging, app design, catalogues & UI

technical

Our technical team develop: wordpress, magento, html5, css3, jQuery, responsive websites, browser compatibility, google apis, cake php, codeigniter

communication

Our communications team speak: social media, mass media, blogs, video, photography, copy writing.
